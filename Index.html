Frequent Questions

Below is a list of some common questions that we have received. Please feel free to contact us if what you're looking for isn't in our FAQ.

What can I do with FancyFonts?

FancyFonts is an online web tool that allows anyone to convert standard text into cool stylish text using the Unicode encoding standard.

What styles does FancyFonts generate?

We currently support over 100 different font styles, including upside-down text, cursive text, small text, gothic text, bold text, italic text, and more. All of these font styles can be copied and pasted into your favorite websites such as Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, Youtube, etc.

What exactly is Unicode?

Unicode is a universal character encoding standard that was created so that a number can represent every unique character around the world. Initially, the internet was based on the English alphabet, which consists of only 128 characters. The standard now supports more than 137,000 characters.

Why am I seeing blank boxes, or question marks?

Not all fonts are supported by every device. If you're trying to view a font and your device doesn't support it, you will see a blank white box or a question mark symbol. Often time this occurs because your device is missing the font file.

Where can I use the fonts generated on FancyFonts?

Some of the most common places to use our text generators are on forums, comments, social media pages, documents, and pretty much anywhere you can paste text.

How can I send feedback to FancyFonts?

Send feedback about our font generators and text tools by contacting: info(at)fancyfonts.co.

About Fancy Fonts

FancyFonts was created to allow people to convert plain text into Unicode encoded characters, which can be copied and pasted into most websites. These font generators will enable you to create unique looking text that's compatible with most modern browsers. It's an excellent way to stand out online.

Our website offers a wide variety of copy and paste text generators. To get started, head over to the text tools navigation and click on any of the links. Once the page loads, enter the text in the input field that is displayed and watch your text get converted into cool funky characters.
